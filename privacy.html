Privacy Policy – Mine Trap Craft 3

Last updated: October 2025

This game collects limited user data to support features such as leaderboards, saved progress, and analytics.

1. Data Collected

  • Player nickname (entered manually by the user)
  • Gameplay statistics such as score, level, and achievements

2. Purpose of Data

Collected data is used only for:

  • Displaying scores on leaderboards
  • Saving player progress
  • Game balancing and analytics

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Sheets via a private script interface. No sensitive information (such as email or IP address) is collected.

4. Third-Party Services

This game uses CrazyGames SDK for ads and analytics. You can read their policy here: CrazyGames Privacy Policy

5. Player Rights

Players may request the deletion of their nickname and score by contacting the developer.
